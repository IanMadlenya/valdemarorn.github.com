## Sinister Mk II - 6 and 7 string

### Completed - May 2017

Specs:

* Black Limba / American Ash Body
* 5 Piece Maple neck with Walnut stringers
* Zebrano / Rosewood fretboard
* Bolt-on Neck
* String-thru body
* Hard-tail bridge
* Aluminium nut
